FRIED CHICKEN

Fried chicken is avery sweet delicious meal good for human health.

ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or wings work best)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour and 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper and 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder and 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for spice) and 2 cups buttermilk (or plain yogurt diluted with water)
  • Cooking oil (vegetable or canola)
